## Visual Style
Editorial data report aesthetic. Cold white background, deep navy and cold blue as the only colors, strict grid layout, typographic hierarchy doing all the work. Authoritative, cold, precise. NOT hand-drawn. NOT warm. NO organic shapes.

## Canvas & Colors
- Background: cold white #F5F7FA
- Primary accent: deep navy #1A3A7A
- Secondary accent: cold blue #2A5CAA
- Light fill: ice blue #EBF0FA
- Body text: dark blue-gray #1A1C2A
- Secondary text: slate #8A9AB0
- Divider lines: 1px cold blue #2A5CAA

## Typography Rules
- Title: ultra-bold, deep navy
- Section labels: bold small caps, letter-spacing 0.15em, cold blue
- Table headers: bold, ice blue fill
- Body: compact regular
- ALL SANS-SERIF GEOMETRIC

## Absolutely No
- Hand-drawn textures or sketch lines
- Warm colors of any kind
- Decorative icons or illustrations
- Colors beyond deep navy + cold blue
