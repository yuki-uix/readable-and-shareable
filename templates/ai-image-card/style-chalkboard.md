# Style: chalkboard

Black chalkboard background with colorful chalk drawing aesthetic. High contrast, educational.

> ⚠️ DALL-E 3 + chalkboard 组合为实验性，渲染结果可能与预期有较大差异。推荐使用 DashScope。

## Canvas

- Ratio: portrait 3:4
- Background: chalkboard black-green (#1A1A1A or #1C2B1C)
- Grid: {N} information zones with chalk-drawn borders

## Typography

- All text in **Simplified Chinese** (简体中文)
- Style: hand-drawn chalk lettering, visible chalk texture
- Imperfect baseline adds authenticity
- Primary text: chalk white #F5F5F5
- Emphasis: bright colored chalk

## Text Constraints (hard limits)

- Zone title: **≤ 8 Chinese characters**
- Zone body: **≤ 20 Chinese characters**
- Do NOT use English `Step N` labels — use `Zone N` or numeric markers (①②③)

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Background | Chalkboard black-green | #1A1A1A / #1C2B1C |
| Primary text | Chalk white | #F5F5F5 |
| Accent 1 | Chalk yellow | #FFE566 |
| Accent 2 | Chalk pink | #FF9999 |
| Accent 3 | Chalk blue | #66B3FF |
| Accent 4 | Chalk green | #90EE90 |

## Decorations

- Chalk dust effects around text and key elements
- Hand-drawn chalk underlines, circles, stars
- Eraser smudge textures for authenticity
- Simple stick-figure icons
- Connection lines with chalk hand-drawn feel

## Attribution Footer

Include at bottom, small chalk text:
```
via readable-and-shareable · {SOURCE_TITLE}
```

## Style Rules

### Do
- Maintain authentic chalk texture on all elements
- Use imperfect, hand-drawn quality throughout
- Add subtle chalk dust and smudge effects
- Create visual hierarchy with color variety
- Include playful chalk doodles and annotations

### Don't
- Use perfect geometric shapes
- Create clean digital-looking lines
- Add photorealistic elements
- Use gradients or glossy effects
- Use a white or light background

## Best Pairings

- Layout: balanced or dense
- Provider: DashScope wanx2.1-t2i-turbo (strongly recommended)
